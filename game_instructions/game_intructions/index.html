How to Play

During this game, you will "gamble" by repeatedly selecting from a choice of two slot machines. Slot machines can win or lose you coins.

One slot machine will, on average, deliver better outcomes than the other. Your goal, for every turn, is to select the better slot machine.

The total number of coins you collect during this game is proportional to the performance bonus (up to $5) you will receive at the end.

How to Play

For simplicity, the slot machines are represented using boxes, with either an "S" or "R" label in the center.

A slot machine's label only indicates the variation in reward, not which arm is better.

"S" boxes are your "Safe" slot machines that will give you the same outcome after every selection.

"R" boxes are your "Risky" slot machines that will give you varying outcomes after every selection.

Again, safe/risky is independent of how rewarding a machine is: a risky machine may deliver more coins on average than a safe machine, and vice versa.

How to Play

Before every turn, you will see the below white cross at the center of the screen.

This cross acts as a reference point for the eye tracker, so try to look at this cross as much as possible.

How to Play

After that, you will see the two slot machines pop up on either side of the cross.

While this cross remains white, you will be unable to select a machine.

How to Play

When the cross turns green, you are able to select one of the two slot machines.

You will have a limited amount of time to make your selection; so, please make your decision as quickly as possible.

Press the left arrow key to select the left slot machine.

Press the right arrow key to select the right slot machine.

How to Play

Once you make your selection, the letter within your selected box will be replaced with a plus or minus sign with a number of coins.

A plus sign means you have won those amount of coins.

A minus sign means you have lost those amount of coins.

How to Play

If you failed to make a selection, the cross will turn red and no outcome will be shown.

Even though no outcome is shown to you, the missed selection will not be treated as a miss, but as if you chose the slot machine with the worst outcome.

If you missed too many selections, we reserve the right to withhold your bonus.

Therefore, please try to pay attention to the task and avoid missing selections.

How to Play

After viewing your outcome, the screen will go blank.

This will only last for brief period of time and you are still actively playing a round of the game.

Soon after the screen goes blank, the white cross will once again appear, indicating your next turn is starting.

How to Play

During the entirety of the experiment, we will be tracking both your pupil size and eye movement.

To make sure we get quality data, please:

  1. Keep your chin placed on the chinrest at all times.
  2. Look at the cross in the middle as much as possible, especially after you have made a selection.
  3. Avoid excessive or prolonged blinking during the task.

How to Play

Don't worry, in between rounds, you will have a chance to rest your eyes and blink for as long and as much as you want.

Even during these breaks, it is still very important you keep your head as still as possible.

After you are done with your break, you can press the space bar to begin your next round.
