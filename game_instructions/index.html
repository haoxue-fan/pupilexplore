How to Play

During this game, you will "gamble" by pulling one lever from a choice of two slot machines. Slot machines can win or lose you coins.

The total number of coins you collect during this game is proportional to the bonus you will earn at the end.

You can earn up to a $5 bonus.

To increase your odds, and unlike a typical slot machine, you will be informed of each slot machine's risk prior to pulling their levers.

How to Play

For simplicity, the slot machines are represented using boxes, with either an "S" or "R" label in the center.

"S" boxes are your "Safe" slot machines that will give you the same outcome for every lever pull.

"R" boxes are your "Risky" slot machines that will give you varying outcomes for every lever pull.

Each round will have two new slot machines.

So, while you will see the same two slot machines for an entire round, this round's slot machines do not affect the next round's slot machines.

At the beginning of each round, the game will tell you what kind of slot machines you will see.

How to Play

Before every new lever pull, you will see the below white cross at the center of the screen.

This cross acts as a reference point, so please try to fix your eyes on this cross as much as possible.

How to Play

After that, you will see the two slot machines pop up on either side of the cross.

While this cross remains white, you will be unable to pull a slot machine lever.

How to Play

When the cross turns green, you are able to pull a lever of one of the two slot machines.

You will have a limited amount of time to make your selection; so, please make your decision as quickly as possible.

Press the left arrow key to pull the lever of the left slot machine.

Press the right arrow key to pull the lever of the right slot machine.

How to Play

Once you make your selection, only the letter within your selected box will be replaced with a plus or minus sign accompanied with a numeric value.

A plus sign means you have won those amount of coins.

A minus sign means you have lost those amount of coins.

How to Play

If you failed to pull a lever, the cross will turn red and no outcome will be shown.

Even though no outcome is shown to you, the missed selection will not actually be treated as a miss, but as if you chose the inferior option.

If you missed too many selections, we reserve the right to withhold your bonus.

Therefore, please try to pay attention to the task and avoid missing selections.

How to Play

After viewing your outcome, the screen will go blank.

Don't worry, this will last for brief period of time and you are still actively playing a round of the game.

Soon after the screen goes blank, the white cross will once again appear, indicating another upcoming lever pull.

How to Play

During the entirety of the experiment, we will be tracking both your pupil size and eye movement.

To make sure we get quality data, please:

  1. Keep your chin placed on the chinrest at all times.
  2. Look at the cross in the middle as much as possible, especially after you have made a selection.
  3. Avoid excessive or prolonged blinking during the task.

How to Play

Don't worry, in between rounds, you will have a chance to rest your eyes and blink for as long and as much as you want.

Even during these breaks, it is still very important you keep your head as still as possible to avoid having to recalibrate the eyetracker.

After you are done with your break, you can click "start" to begin the new round.

Some Game Hints

  1. Because missed lever pulls always give you the inferior option, it increases your chance at a higher bonus if you always pull a lever.
  2. The amount of coins that a machine will give you is not related to its position on the screen.
  3. Each round has two new slot machines and therefore two new outcomes.
  4. "S" slot machines do not guarentee a positive reward. "S" only indicates that you will receive the same outcome throughout a round.
  5. "R" slot machines do not guarentee a negative reward. "R" only indicates that you will receive varying outcomes throughout a round.
